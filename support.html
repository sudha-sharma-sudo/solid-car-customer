Frequently Asked Questions

Making a reservation is easy! Simply browse our available cars, select your desired dates, and follow the booking process. You'll need to be logged in to complete the reservation.

You'll need to present a valid driver's license, credit card, and booking confirmation. International customers must also show their passport.

You can modify or cancel your booking through your dashboard up to 24 hours before the pickup time. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
